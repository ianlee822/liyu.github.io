I am a computer vision researcher. I had a great opportunity of working at Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a research center founded by University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) and the Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore during 2014-2016. I got my PhD degree from School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2015, working with Prof. Michael S. Brown. My thesis has won the IDA medal (best thesis award)
